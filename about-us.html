Quick Introduction

Our HQ is located in Manhattan, NYC. But have offices and presences in UK, Canada, Japan and Germany.

Our game studio is a group of passionate developers who share a love for video games and a desire to create amazing gaming experiences. With expertise in game design, programming, and art, we work together to deliver high-quality games that are engaging, challenging, and fun to play. We believe in pushing the boundaries of what can be done in games, and we strive to create titles that are both innovative and entertaining. Our mission is to develop games that players love, and we are committed to excellence in every aspect of game development.
